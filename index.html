Helpful Answers

Everything you might ask

What is your cancellation policy?+

Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to fourteen days before arrival. Cancellations within fourteen days are charged the first night; no-shows are charged the full stay. Suites and villas booked under our Best Available Rate may be cancelled until 48 hours before arrival. We always recommend travel insurance.

What ages are admitted?+

Our property is reserved for adult guests aged 18 and over, in line with our gaming licence and the character of the resort. Valid government-issued photo identification may be requested on arrival. Children are warmly welcomed at our sister property in the same bay — the concierge will be happy to arrange the booking and transfers between the two estates.

Can you host private events?+

Yes. Our two ballrooms, eight private dining rooms, beach pavilion, and rotunda are available for private hire, with capacities from eight to four hundred guests. Weddings and milestone celebrations are a particular pleasure of ours; our events director will design a proposal within forty-eight hours of an initial enquiry.

How far is the airport?+

Approximately thirty-five minutes by private car along the coastal road in normal conditions, or fifty-five minutes if your driver takes the scenic inland route through the vineyards (we recommend this on arrival days when there is no hurry). By helicopter from the resort helipad, the journey is twelve minutes door-to-door.

A Note From The Owners

Built one room at a time.

When we opened in 1981 we had six rooms, a kitchen, and one rule: guests are not units of revenue, they are people we have invited home. 45 years later we have 120 keys, three restaurants and a casino floor — and the rule has not moved an inch.

There is a way to do this work and a way not to. We have, over 45 years, picked one and stuck to it. Every member of our team — from the front desk to the kitchen porters — has been chosen, trained and kept because they share the same instinct about what good looks like. That is not something a building can produce on its own. It comes from people.

We hope you will come and see for yourself. And, when you do, we hope you will tell us where we got it wrong, so that the next 45 years are better than the last.

L. Marchetti · H. Albright
